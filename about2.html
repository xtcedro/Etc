My Journey and Mission

My journey has been one of growth, resilience, and a drive to make a difference. I was brought to Oklahoma City as a young child on December 19, 1999, and this city has been the foundation of my dreams and ambitions ever since. Graduating from Emerson High School taught me the power of perseverance, and my current work at McDonald’s Inc. has instilled in me a strong work ethic and a commitment to excellence. Now, I’m ready to take the next step in my journey: dedicating myself full-time to empowering local businesses and individuals in the Oklahoma City metropolitan area through innovative and accessible IT solutions. I believe every challenge is a chance to rise higher, and I am committed to using my skills and passion to create technology that inspires growth, fosters success, and transforms lives. Together, we can shape a brighter future, one solution at a time.

My Catholic faith is the bedrock of my life. Rooted in the teachings of Christ, I strive to live out the values of integrity, compassion, and service. These principles, inspired by the call to love our neighbors as ourselves (Mark 12:31), are at the heart of my mission to empower businesses and individuals through technology.

Over the years, I’ve gained extensive experience solving technical problems, designing user-friendly websites, and managing complex server setups. Through it all, I am guided by the Church’s teachings on stewardship and the dignity of work. Like St. Joseph the Worker, I believe that every task—big or small—should reflect our dedication to excellence and service to God.

"Faith is not just a belief; it is a call to action. With trust in God and a heart devoted to service, I strive to make technology a force for good in the lives of those around me."

Whether you need a solution for your business or assistance with a personal project, my goal is to deliver transparent, affordable, and secure services that align with the values of our Catholic faith. Together, we can build technology that serves the common good and reflects our shared commitment to faith and innovation.
